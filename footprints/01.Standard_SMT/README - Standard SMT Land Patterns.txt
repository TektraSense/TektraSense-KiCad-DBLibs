IPC-7351 Land Pattern Naming Convention Notes
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ï¿½ All dimensions are in Metric Units
ï¿½ All Lead Span and Height numbers go two places past the decimal point and ï¿½includeï¿½ trailing Zeros
ï¿½ All Lead Span and Body Sizes go two place before the decimal point and ï¿½removeï¿½ leading Zeros
ï¿½ All Chip Component Body Sizes are one place to each side of the decimal point
ï¿½ Pitch Values are two places to the right & left of decimal point with no leading Zeros but include trailing zeros
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Naming Convention Special Character Use for Land Patterns
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ï¿½ The _ (underscore) is the separator between pin Qty in Hidden & Deleted pin components.
ï¿½ The ï¿½ (dash) is used to separate the pin qty.
ï¿½ The X (capital letter X) is used instead of the word ï¿½byï¿½ to separate two numbers such as height X width like ï¿½Quad Packagesï¿½.

IPC-7251B Suffix Naming convention for Land Patterns
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Common SMT Land Pattern to Describe Environment Use (This is the last character in every name)
Note: This excludes the BGA component family as they only come in the Nominal Environment Condition
  ï¿½ M ................. Most Material Condition 	(Level A)
  ï¿½ N.................. Nominal Material Condition 	(Level B)
  ï¿½ L ................. Least Material Condition 	(Level C)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Alternate Components that do not follow the JEDEC, EIA or IEC Standard
  ï¿½ A.................. Alternate Component (used primarily for SOP & QFP when Component Tolerance or Height is different)
  ï¿½ B.................. Second Alternate Component
  ï¿½ D.................. Refer from Datasheet
  ï¿½ J.................. Refer from NDR (JAPAN)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Reverse Pin Order
  ï¿½ -20RN ............. 20 pin part, Reverse Pin Order, Nominal Environment
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Hidden Pins
  ï¿½ -20_24N ........... 20 pin part in a 24 pin package. The pins are numbered 1 ï¿½ 24 the hidden pins are skipped. The schematic
symbol displays up to 24 pins.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Deleted Pins
  ï¿½ -24_20N ........... 20 pin part in a 24 pin package. The pins are numbered 1 ï¿½ 20. The schematic symbol displays 20 pins.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
JEDEC and EIA Standard parts that have several alternate packages
  ï¿½ AA, AB, AC . JEDEC or EIA Component Identifier

GENERAL SUFFIXES
_HS  .................. HS  = Land Pattern with Heat Sink attachment requiring additional holes or pads
                              Example: TO254P1055X160_HS-6N
_BEC .................. BEC = Base, Emitter and Collector (Pin assignments used for three pin Transistors)
                              Example: SOT95P280X160_BEC-3N
_SGD .................. SGD = Source, Gate and Drain (Pin assignments used for three pin Transistors)
                              Example: SOT95P280X160_SGD-3N
_213 .................. 213 = Alternate pin assignments used for three pin Transistors
                              Example: SOT95P280X160_213-3N
_D or J ...............	D   = Refer from Datasheet or Pattern/soldering Recommendation in Datasheet
                              Example: XCVR_ESP32-C3-MINI-1-N4_D 	
_J or J ...............	J   = Recommendation from NDR (Japan) or Old PCB Layout from Japan Old Design.
                              Example: RESC_RK73B1ETTP104J_J
